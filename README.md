# Awesome WearOS [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

A curated list of awesome WearOS apps

## Apps
 * [Samsung Internet Browser](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.sec.android.app.sbrowser) - Access the internet right from your watch
 * [Stratum](https://github.com/stratumauth/app) - Show your 2FA codes on your watch for quick access 
 * [Shazam](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.shazam.android) - Identify nearby songs
  * [Macrodroid](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.arlosoft.macrodroid) - Make your own automations for wearos (does not work without your phone)
  * [Pixel Compass](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.github.fertwbr.pixelcompass) - A material design compass and water leveler
  * [Home Assistant](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.homeassistant.companion.android) - Allows you to enable watch based sensors and control your home assistant instance right from your wrist
  * [Whatsapp](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.whatsapp) - Full access to whatsapp on your watch

## Watchfaces
  * [Pujie Watch Faces - WearOS 6](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.pujie.watchfaces) - Community made watchfaces with support for WearOS 6
  * [Pujie Watch Faces - WearOS 4-](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.pujie.wristwear.pujieblack) - Community made watchfaces with support for WearOS versions lower than 6
## Complications
  * [Phone Battery Complication](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.weartools.phonebattcomp) - See your phone battery right from your watchface

## Tiles
  * [Favorite Applications](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.rayadams.favapps) - Quickly acces your favorite apps from a tile
## Utilities
  * [GeminiMan WearOS Manager](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.geminiman.wearosmanager) - Allows you to send ADB commands and sideload apps onto your watch
